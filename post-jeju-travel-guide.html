🏝️ Top Attractions

🏔️

Hallasan National Park

Korea's highest peak (1,950m) — a dormant volcano with a crater lake at the summit. Multiple hiking trails from 3 to 9 hours.

🕳️

Manjanggul Lava Tube

One of the world's longest lava tubes — a 13km underground cave system formed by ancient volcanic activity. UNESCO listed.

🌊

Hyeopjae Beach

Pristine white sand and turquoise water — Jeju's most beautiful beach, backed by a pine forest and a lava rock shoreline.

🌺

Seongsan Ilchulbong

"Sunrise Peak" — a dramatic volcanic tuff cone rising from the sea. The sunrise view from the top is one of Korea's most iconic sights.

🍊

Tangerine Farms (감귤밭)

Jeju is famous for its sweet tangerines — visit a farm in autumn (Oct–Dec) to pick and eat them straight from the tree.

🏖️

Jungmun Resort Area

Jeju's upscale resort strip — luxury hotels, Cheonjeyeon Waterfall, Yeomiji Botanical Garden and the famous Jusangjeolli lava cliffs.

🗺️ Jeju by Area

Jeju Island is roughly divided into east, west, north and south — plan your days by area to avoid excessive driving.

📍 Jeju City (North)

  • Yongduam Rock (Dragon Head Rock)
  • Jeju Folk Village Museum
  • Dongmun Traditional Market
  • Jeju Airport — main entry point

📍 East Jeju

  • Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak)
  • Manjanggul Lava Tube
  • Seopjikoji Coastal Road
  • Haenyeo (female diver) shows

📍 West Jeju

  • Hyeopjae Beach & Hallim Park
  • Olle Trail sections (7 & 14)
  • Biyangdo Island day trip
  • Aewol Handam Coastal Road

📍 South Jeju

  • Jungmun Resort & waterfall
  • Jusangjeolli Lava Cliffs
  • Hallasan hiking (Eorimok trail)
  • Seogwipo City seafood market

🍽️ Jeju Food Specialties

🐷

Heuk Dwaeji (흑돼지)

Jeju's black pork — richer flavor than regular pork. Grilled at the table and eaten with island-grown vegetables.

🦑

Haenyeo Seafood (해녀 음식)

Dishes made from ingredients harvested by Jeju's famous female divers — abalone, sea urchin, conch.

🍊

Hallabong (한라봉)

Jeju's prized citrus fruit — sweeter than a regular tangerine, knobby on top. Available Nov–Feb.

🍺

Jeju Sool (제주술)

Jeju craft beer and soju made from local ingredients — try at the many Jeju-themed breweries.

🍡

Ome (오메기떡)

Traditional Jeju rice cake made from foxtail millet — chewy, nutty, a beloved local snack.

🦐

Galchi Jorim (갈치조림)

Braised hairtail fish — a Jeju specialty slow-cooked in spicy soy sauce with vegetables.

✈️ Getting to Jeju

✈️

Flight — Only Realistic Option

There's no train or bridge to Jeju. Flights from Gimpo (Seoul) take ~1 hour. Flights from Incheon take ~1h 10min. Check Korean Air, Asiana, Jeju Air, T'way, Jin Air for cheap fares. Book early — Jeju route is one of the world's busiest.

🚢

Ferry (Slow but Scenic)

Mokpo → Jeju: ~5 hours by high-speed ferry. Wando → Jeju: ~2.5 hours. Popular with cyclists and those bringing cars. Book via Korean Ferry app.

🚗 Getting Around Jeju

Transport Options

  • Rental Car — Best option: Jeju's public transport is limited. Renting a car gives full freedom. International driving permit required. Book in advance.
  • Scooter/E-bike rental: Good for coastal roads and Olle Trails. Available in Jeju City and Seogwipo.
  • Bus: Intercity buses connect major attractions but are infrequent (1–2 per hour). Use Naver Maps for routes.
  • Taxi: Available and reasonably priced. Jeju taxis are metered — no scams.

📅 Best Time to Visit Jeju

By Season

  • Spring (Mar–May) — Canola flower fields turn yellow (February peak), cherry blossoms, mild weather
  • Summer (Jun–Aug) — Beach season, hot and humid, rainy season in late June–July
  • Autumn (Sep–Nov) — Best hiking weather, tangerine harvest begins in October, stunning foliage
  • Winter (Dec–Feb) — Fewest tourists, cheapest prices, Hallasan may have snow, Hallabong citrus season
💡 Jeju Travel Tips:
• Jeju is visa-free for most nationalities (even if you need a visa for mainland Korea) — check this before planning.
• Download Naver Maps and Kakao Maps — both work better than Google Maps on the island.
• Book accommodation in advance during May (canola season) and October (autumn) — it fills up fast.
