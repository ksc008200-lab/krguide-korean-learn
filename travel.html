Best Time to Visit Korea

Korea has four distinct seasons — each completely different from the last.

🌸

Spring

March — May

Cherry blossoms peak in early April. Perfect weather (15–22°C). Most popular season — book accommodation early.

☀️

Summer

June — August

Hot and humid (30°C+). Monsoon rains mid-July to mid-August. Beach season in Busan. Festivals and outdoor events.

🍂

Autumn

September — November

Best weather of the year (15–25°C). Stunning fall foliage. Hiking season. Busan Film Festival in October.

❄️

Winter

December — February

Cold but dry (-5 to 5°C). Ski resorts open. Fewer tourists, cheaper prices. Hallabong citrus season in Jeju.
