## Korean Family Values · 한국 가족 가치관

> 🎯 **효 (Hyo) — Filial Piety** — The supreme virtue. Children honor and care for parents lifelong.
>
> 🎯 **장남 (Jangnam) — Eldest Son** — Traditionally responsible for ancestral rites. Modern law has equalized inheritance.
>
> 🎯 **세배 (Sebae) — New Year Bow** — On Lunar New Year, children bow deeply to elders and receive 세뱃돈.
>
> 🎯 **명절 (Myeongjeol) — Family Holidays** — Chuseok and Seollal are mandatory family gatherings.
