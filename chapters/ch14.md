*Chapter 14 · 제14장*
# Question Words — Ask Anything in Korean!
**의문사 — 어떤 질문도 한국어로!**

의문사 · ui-mun-sa · The Key to Every Conversation

💡 **Tip:** The ability to ask questions is what makes conversation real. Once you know these 7 question words, you can ask about anything — people, places, times, reasons, methods. Every great conversation starts with a question. 🗣️

## The 7 Essential Question Words · 7가지 핵심 의문사

| # | Korean | Romanization | English | Example |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 누구 | nu-gu | who | 이 사람 누구예요? — Who is this? |
| 2 | 무엇 / 뭐 | mu-eot / mwo | what | 이거 뭐예요? — What is this? |
| 3 | 어디 | eo-di | where | 어디 가요? — Where are you going? |
| 4 | 언제 | eon-je | when | 언제 만나요? — When do we meet? |
| 5 | 왜 | wae | why | 왜 그래요? — Why is that? |
| 6 | 어떻게 | eo-tteo-ke | how | 어떻게 가요? — How do I get there? |
| 7 | 얼마 / 몇 | eol-ma / myeot | how much / how many | 얼마예요? — How much is it? |

## 1 · 누구 (Who)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 이 사람 누구예요? | Who is this person? |
| 누가 왔어요? | Who came? (누가 = 누구 + subject) |
| 누구랑 가요? | Who are you going with? |
| 누구한테 줄 거예요? | Whom will you give it to? |

## 2 · 무엇 / 뭐 (What)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 이름이 뭐예요? | What's your name? |
| 뭐 먹어요? | What are you eating? |
| 뭐 좋아해요? | What do you like? |
| 무슨 일이에요? | What's the matter? |
| 무슨 색깔이에요? | What color is it? |

## 3 · 어디 (Where)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 어디 가요? | Where are you going? |
| 어디에서 왔어요? | Where did you come from? |
| 화장실 어디예요? | Where is the bathroom? |
| 어디 살아요? | Where do you live? |
| 어디에 있어요? | Where is it? |

## 4 · 언제 (When)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 언제 만나요? | When do we meet? |
| 언제 와요? | When are you coming? |
| 언제부터? | Since when? |
| 언제까지? | Until when? |
| 생일 언제예요? | When is your birthday? |

## 5 · 왜 (Why)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 왜요? | Why? |
| 왜 그래요? | Why are you like that? |
| 왜 늦었어요? | Why are you late? |
| 왜 안 와요? | Why aren't you coming? |
| 왜 그렇게 생각해요? | Why do you think so? |

## 6 · 어떻게 (How)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 어떻게 가요? | How do I go? |
| 어떻게 알아요? | How do you know? |
| 이거 어떻게 해요? | How do I do this? |
| 어떻게 됐어요? | How did it go? |
| 한국말 어떻게 배워요? | How do I learn Korean? |

## 7 · 얼마 / 몇 (How much / How many)

얼마 = for prices and uncountable amounts. 몇 = for counting things with a counter.

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 얼마예요? | How much is it? |
| 얼마나 걸려요? | How long does it take? |
| 몇 시예요? | What time is it? |
| 몇 살이에요? | How old are you? |
| 몇 명이에요? | How many people? |
| 몇 개 드릴까요? | How many would you like? |

## Combo Question Forms · 의문사 조합

| Combo | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 무슨 + noun | what kind of | 무슨 음식? — What food? |
| 어느 + noun | which | 어느 나라? — Which country? |
| 어떤 + noun | what kind of | 어떤 사람? — What kind of person? |
| 누구의 + noun | whose | 누구의 책? — Whose book? |
| 어디 + noun | where + noun | 어디 식당? — Which restaurant? |

## Real Conversation Practice · 실전 대화 연습

> 처음 만난 사이
> A: 이름이 뭐예요? (What's your name?)
> B: 제이슨이에요. 어디에서 오셨어요? (I'm Jason. Where are you from?)
> A: 한국 사람이에요. 한국 와본 적 있어요? (I'm Korean. Have you been to Korea?)
> B: 네, 작년에 한 번. (Yes, once last year.)

> 길에서
> A: 화장실 어디예요? (Where's the bathroom?)
> B: 저쪽으로 가세요. (Go that way.)
> A: 얼마나 걸려요? (How long does it take?)
> B: 1분이요. (One minute.)

> 💡 Master tip: Memorize the 7 question words today. Then for one week, ask every Korean person you meet at least 3 questions using them. By day 7, asking will feel automatic.
