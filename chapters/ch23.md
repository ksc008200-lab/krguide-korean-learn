# K-pop & Korean Entertainment — Hallyu Wave!
**K팝과 한국 엔터테인먼트 — 한류의 물결!**

K팝 & 한국 엔터테인먼트 · K-pop & han-guk en-teo-tein-meon-teu · Ride the Korean Wave!

💡 **Tip:** 한류 (Hallyu) changed the world! The Korean Wave — K-pop, K-dramas, Korean movies, Korean food, Korean beauty — has conquered the globe. Most people who learn Korean today do so because of 한류. Now let's learn the language behind the wave — the real Korean used in songs, dramas, and fan culture! 🎵📺

## The Hallyu Wave · 한류의 모든 것

> 🎵 **K-pop (케이팝)** — BTS, BLACKPINK, NewJeans, Stray Kids, IVE, LE SSERAFIM lead the global wave.
> 📺 **K-dramas (드라마)** — Squid Game, Crash Landing on You, Goblin, Reply 1988, Itaewon Class.
> 🎬 **K-cinema (영화)** — Parasite (Oscar winner), Decision to Leave, Train to Busan, Old Boy.
> 💄 **K-beauty (뷰티)** — Glass skin (유리피부), 10-step skincare, BB cream — invented in Korea.
> 🎮 **K-gaming (게임)** — League of Legends pro scene, PUBG, Maple Story.

## Cultural Notes · 문화 노트

> 💜 **응원봉 (Lightstick)** — Each group has its own unique lightstick. Owning one signals which fandom you belong to.

> 🎤 **음방 (Music shows)** — M Countdown, Music Bank, Show Champion, Music Core, Inkigayo. K-pop groups perform weekly comebacks here.

> 🏆 **연말 시상식 (Year-end awards)** — MAMA, MMA, Golden Disc, Seoul Music Awards. The K-pop equivalent of the Grammys.

> 💡 K-pop fluency tip: pick ONE group, watch their reality content, learn their slang. You'll absorb Korean naturally through love.
