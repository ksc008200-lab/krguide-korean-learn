*Chapter 19 · 제19장*
# Weather & Seasons — Talk Like a Korean!
**날씨와 계절 — 한국인처럼 대화하기**

날씨 & 계절 · nal-ssi & gye-jeol · Korea's 4 Distinct Seasons!

💡 **Tip:** Weather is the #1 conversation starter! Koreans talk about weather constantly — it's the perfect small talk topic. Korea has 4 beautifully distinct seasons: cherry blossoms in spring 🌸, hot humid summers ☀️, breathtaking autumn foliage 🍁, and cold snowy winters ❄️. Learning weather vocabulary opens up endless conversations!

## The Four Seasons · 사계절

| Korean | Romanization | English | Months |
|---|---|---|---|
| 봄 | bom | spring | 3월–5월 |
| 여름 | yeo-reum | summer | 6월–8월 |
| 가을 | ga-eul | autumn | 9월–11월 |
| 겨울 | gyeo-ul | winter | 12월–2월 |

## Conversation Starters · 대화 시작 표현

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 오늘 날씨 좋네요! | The weather is nice today! |
| 너무 더워요. | It's so hot. |
| 비가 와요. | It's raining. |
| 우산 가져오세요. | Bring an umbrella. |
| 날씨가 갑자기 추워졌어요. | The weather suddenly got cold. |
| 미세먼지가 심해요. | The fine dust is bad. |
| 단풍이 예뻐요. | The autumn leaves are pretty. |
| 첫눈이 왔어요! | The first snow has come! |

## Seasonal Korean Culture · 계절별 한국 문화

> 🌸 **봄 (Spring)** — Cherry blossom festivals, picnics in 여의도 and 진해
> ☀️ **여름 (Summer)** — Beach trips, 삼복 (3 hottest days), 삼계탕 chicken soup ritual
> 🍁 **가을 (Autumn)** — 단풍 leaf-viewing, harvest 추석 holiday
> ❄️ **겨울 (Winter)** — Skiing, hot 어묵 from street stalls, 김장 kimchi-making

> 🌸 **봄** — 벚꽃 축제, 여의도·진해의 봄 소풍
> ☀️ **여름** — 해수욕장, 삼복더위, 삼계탕 풍습
> 🍁 **가을** — 단풍 구경, 추석 명절
> ❄️ **겨울** — 스키, 길거리 어묵, 김장철

> 💡 Korean weather forecast on phone: 날씨 (Naver Weather) is the most-used app. Know 미세먼지 levels — it affects whether Koreans go outside.
