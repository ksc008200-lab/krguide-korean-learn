## Sample Dialogues · 예시 대화

> **A:** 취미가 뭐예요? (What's your hobby?)
> **B:** 저는 등산을 좋아해요. 매주 토요일에 산에 가요. (I like hiking. I go to the mountains every Saturday.)
> **A:** 어디 자주 가요? (Where do you often go?)
> **B:** 북한산이요. 서울에서 가까워서 좋아요. (Bukhansan. It's close to Seoul.)

> **A:** 시간 있을 때 뭐 해요? (What do you do in your free time?)
> **B:** 보통 영화 봐요. 액션 영화 좋아해요. (Usually watch movies. I like action films.)
> **A:** 최근에 본 영화 추천해 주세요. (Recommend a movie you watched recently.)
> **B:** 한국 영화 "범죄도시 5" 진짜 재미있어요. ("The Outlaws 5" is really fun.)

## Pro Tip · 고급 팁

> 🎯 **Find your 동호회 (hobby club)**
>
> Korea has 동호회 (hobby groups) for almost every interest — hiking, photography, language exchange, board games. They're a great way to:
> - Practice Korean with locals
> - Make Korean friends
> - Experience real culture beyond tourist spots
>
> Search 네이버 카페, 당근마켓 동네생활, or Meetup Korea for hobby groups in your area.

> 💡 Talking about hobbies in Korean is a fast-track to friendship. Memorize 5 hobby words and 3 sample answers — you're set for any first conversation.
