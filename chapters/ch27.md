# Travel in Korea — Explore Every Corner!
**한국 여행 — 구석구석 둘러보기!**

한국 여행 · han-guk yeo-haeng · From Seoul to Jeju — See It All!

💡 **Tip:** Korea is one of the most travel-friendly countries in the world — safe, clean, with world-class infrastructure. From neon-lit Seoul streets to volcanic Jeju, every corner has something magical. ✈️🏔️🌊

## Visa & Entry · 비자와 입국

| Country | Visa-Free Stay |
|---|---|
| USA | 90 days (with K-ETA) |
| EU countries | 90 days (with K-ETA) |
| Japan | 90 days (with K-ETA) |
| China | Visa required |

> 💡 K-ETA (Korea Electronic Travel Authorization) is required for visa-free entry — apply online 24+ hours before flight. ₩10,000 fee, valid 3 years.

## Emergency Numbers for Travelers · 여행자 응급 번호

| Number | Service |
|---|---|
| 1330 | Tourism info (24/7, English/Japanese/Chinese) |
| 112 | Police |
| 119 | Fire / Ambulance |
