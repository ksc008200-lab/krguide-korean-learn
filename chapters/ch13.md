*Chapter 13 · 제13장*
# Particles — The Magic Glue of Korean!
**조사 — 한국어를 자연스럽게 만드는 비밀**

조사 · jo-sa · The Secret to Sounding Natural

💡 **Tip:** In English, word order tells you who does what. In Korean, particles (조사) are tiny words attached to nouns that mark their role — subject, object, location, and more. This means Korean word order is flexible! 🤯

## 1 · Topic & Subject Particles · 주제/주어 조사

| Particle | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 은 (after consonant) | topic marker | 저**는** 학생이에요 — As for me, I'm a student |
| 는 (after vowel) | topic marker | 친구**는** 가요 — My friend is going |
| 이 (after consonant) | subject marker | 책**이** 있어요 — There is a book |
| 가 (after vowel) | subject marker | 친구**가** 왔어요 — A friend came |

> 📌 **Rule: 은/는 vs 이/가**
> 은/는 → topic ("as for X..."), often previously known information
> 이/가 → grammatical subject, often newly introduced information

## 2 · Object Particle · 목적어 조사 · 을/를

> 🔧 **Formula** — What is being acted upon?
> [Subject] + [Object + 을/를] + [Verb]
> 을 → after consonant · 를 → after vowel

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 사과**를** 먹어요 | I eat an apple |
| 책**을** 읽어요 | I read a book |
| 커피**를** 마셔요 | I drink coffee |
| 음악**을** 들어요 | I listen to music |
| 영화**를** 봐요 | I watch a movie |

## 3 · Location Particles · 장소 조사 · 에 / 에서

| Particle | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 에 | direction / time / existence | 학교**에** 가요 (go to school) |
| 에서 | location of action | 카페**에서** 공부해요 (study at the café) |

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 집**에** 있어요 | I'm at home |
| 학교**에** 가요 | I go to school |
| 도서관**에서** 공부해요 | I study at the library |
| 식당**에서** 밥 먹어요 | I eat at the restaurant |
| 7시**에** 만나요 | Meet at 7 o'clock |

## 4 · Connection Particles · 연결 조사 · 와/과, 하고, 랑

| Particle | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 와 (after vowel) | and / with (formal) | 친구**와** 만나요 |
| 과 (after consonant) | and / with (formal) | 책**과** 펜 |
| 하고 | and / with (neutral) | 친구**하고** 가요 |
| (이)랑 | and / with (casual) | 친구**랑** 놀아요 |

## 5 · Honorific Particles · 존경 조사

| Particle | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 께서 | honorific subject | 선생님**께서** 오셨어요 |
| 께 | honorific recipient | 어머니**께** 선물을 드렸어요 |

## 6 · Other Useful Particles · 기타 핵심 조사

| Particle | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 도 | also / too | 저**도** 좋아요 (Me too) |
| 만 | only | 물**만** 마셔요 (I drink only water) |
| 부터 | from (time/place) | 9시**부터** 일해요 (I work from 9) |
| 까지 | until / to | 5시**까지** 일해요 (I work until 5) |
| 보다 | than | 친구**보다** 키가 커요 (taller than my friend) |
| 으로 / 로 | by means of | 버스**로** 가요 (by bus) |
