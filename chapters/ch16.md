# Shopping & Money — Shop Like a Korean!
**쇼핑과 돈 — 한국인처럼 쇼핑하기!**

쇼핑 & 돈 · syo-ping & don · From Markets to Malls!

💡 **Tip:** Korea is a shopper's paradise! From the giant malls of 명동 (Myeongdong) to local 시장 (traditional markets), Korea has it all. Knowing how to ask prices, bargain politely, and pay will make your experience 10x more fun. 🛍️

## Korean Money · 한국 돈 (원 · won)

The Korean currency is 원 (won). There are no cents — everything is in whole numbers. ₩10,000 ≈ about $7–8 USD.

| Denomination | Korean | Color / Person |
|---|---|---|
| ₩10 | 십 원 | Bronze coin |
| ₩50 | 오십 원 | Silver coin |
| ₩100 | 백 원 | Silver — King Yi Sun-sin |
| ₩500 | 오백 원 | Silver — crane |
| ₩1,000 | 천 원 | Blue — Yi Hwang (scholar) |
| ₩5,000 | 오천 원 | Red — Yi I (scholar) |
| ₩10,000 | 만 원 | Green — King Sejong |
| ₩50,000 | 오만 원 | Yellow — Shin Saimdang |

> 💳 Modern Korea is largely **cashless** — credit/debit cards, T-Money transit cards, and KakaoPay dominate. Most shops accept cards even for ₩1,000 purchases.

## Big Number Reading · 큰 숫자 읽기

Korean uses 10,000 (만) as a base unit, not 1,000. This trips up English speakers.

| Number | Korean | English |
|---|---|---|
| ₩1,000 | 천 원 | 1 thousand won |
| ₩10,000 | 만 원 | 10 thousand won |
| ₩100,000 | 십만 원 | 100 thousand won |
| ₩1,000,000 | 백만 원 | 1 million won |
| ₩10,000,000 | 천만 원 | 10 million won |
| ₩100,000,000 | 일억 원 | 100 million won |

> 💡 Trick: 만 (10,000) = 1 "Korean unit". Apartments cost "5억" (₩500 million ≈ $370k). Cars cost "3천만" (₩30 million ≈ $22k).

## Bargaining Tips · 흥정 팁

> ✅ Bargaining WORKS at:
> - Traditional markets (남대문, 동대문, 광장시장)
> - Underground shopping arcades
> - Some Gyeongdong herbal market shops
>
> ❌ Bargaining DOES NOT work at:
> - Department stores
> - Chain stores / franchises
> - Online platforms
> - Government / luxury shops

> 💡 Polite bargaining phrases:
> - "좀 깎아 주세요" — Please give me a discount
> - "조금만 더 깎아 주실 수 있어요?" — Can you discount it a little more?
> - "현금으로 하면 깎아 주세요" — Discount if I pay cash

> 💡 한국 쇼핑은 매우 효율적. 카드 결제 + 빠른 배송 + 30일 환불이 표준입니다.
