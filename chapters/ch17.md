# Transportation & Directions — Navigate Korea!
**교통과 길 찾기 — 한국을 누비자!**

교통 & 길 찾기 · gyo-tong & gil chat-gi · Get Anywhere in Korea!

💡 **Tip:** Korea has world-class public transport! Seoul's subway covers the entire city, buses go everywhere, and KTX high-speed trains connect all major cities. Once you learn these phrases, you can travel anywhere in Korea independently. 🚇🚌🚅

## Types of Transport · 교통수단

> 🚇 **지하철 (Subway)** — The backbone of Seoul. 1-9 lines + numbered extensions.
> 🚌 **버스 (Bus)** — Cheap, frequent, color-coded (blue=mainline, green=neighborhood, red=express).
> 🚕 **택시 (Taxi)** — Orange/silver = standard, black = premium, free of Uber-style apps but 카카오 T is the equivalent.
> 🚅 **KTX (고속철도)** — Seoul to Busan in 2.5 hours.
> ✈️ **비행기 (Airplane)** — 김포공항 (domestic), 인천공항 (international).
> 🚶 **도보 (Walking)** — Naver Map or Kakao Map for directions (better than Google in Korea).

## Pro Tips · 고급 팁

> 📱 **Apps you NEED:**
> - **카카오 T** — Taxi, navigation, public transit
> - **네이버 지도 / 카카오맵** — Maps (better than Google in Korea)
> - **지하철 노선도** — Subway map app
> - **티머니 (T-Money)** — Transit card; tap your phone

> 💳 **T-Money card** — Buy at any convenience store (₩2,500). Tap on subways, buses, taxis. Recharge anywhere. Foreigners can buy without registration.

> 🚖 **Late-night surcharge** — Taxi rates increase 20% from midnight to 4 AM.

> 🚄 **KTX booking** — Use KORAIL app or Naver Pay. Tickets sell out for weekends — book 2 days ahead.
