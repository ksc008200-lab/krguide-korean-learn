# Body & Health — Stay Safe in Korea!
**신체와 건강 — 한국에서 안전하게 지내기!**

신체 & 건강 · sin-che & geon-gang · Essential for Every Traveler!

💡 **Tip:** Medical Korean could save your life! Knowing how to say "my head hurts," "I have a fever," or "I'm allergic to this" is essential. Korea has excellent hospitals, but staff may not always speak English. 🏥

## Emergency Phrases · 응급 표현

> 🚨 **In an emergency, dial 119** (ambulance/fire) or 112 (police)
> Korea's emergency number for foreign-language assistance: **1330** (Tourism Information, 24/7 English/Japanese/Chinese)

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 도와주세요! | Help! |
| 119에 전화해 주세요 | Please call 119 |
| 응급실 어디예요? | Where's the ER? |
| 외국인이에요 | I'm a foreigner |
| 영어 할 줄 아는 의사 있어요? | Is there an English-speaking doctor? |
| 알레르기가 있어요 | I have allergies |
| 임신 중이에요 | I'm pregnant |
| 기저질환이 있어요 | I have a pre-existing condition |

## Korean Health Insurance for Foreigners · 외국인 건강보험

If you stay in Korea for **6+ months**, you can/must enroll in 국민건강보험 (National Health Insurance). Premiums are ~₩100,000–150,000/month, and medical costs drop to ~1/3 of normal price.

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 국민건강보험 | National Health Insurance |
| 건강보험증 | Insurance card |
| 가입하다 | enroll |
| 보험료 | premium |

> 💡 한국 의료 시스템은 세계 최고 수준 + 비교적 저렴. 건강보험에 가입했다면 응급실도 ₩30,000~50,000 수준입니다.
